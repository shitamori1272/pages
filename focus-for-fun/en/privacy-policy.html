Privacy Policy

The FocusForFun app (hereinafter referred to as "this app") values the protection of users' personal information and privacy, and establishes the following privacy policy.

Last updated: April 8, 2025

Collection of Personal Information

This app accesses users' Apple Screen Time (a function that records and manages usage data and app usage time) and HealthKit (a framework for safely storing and managing healthcare data) data, but all this information is processed on the user's device and is not transmitted or collected externally by this app.

This app does not collect any personal information that could identify users.

Purpose of Data Usage

The Screen Time and HealthKit data accessed by this app is used only for the following purposes:

  • To show the user's progress toward achieving their goals
  • To perform processing within the scope necessary for the app's functionality

This data is processed only on the device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Collection and Use of Anonymous Information

For future service improvement and app enhancement, we may collect and analyze anonymous information that cannot identify individuals. The anonymous information collected may include app crash status and usage status, but does not include any information that could identify individuals.

Data Management and Deletion

Data processed by this app is stored on the device, and users can revoke the app's access permissions to Screen Time and HealthKit from iOS standard settings at any time. Additionally, all app-related data will be deleted when the app is uninstalled.

Disclaimer

While we provide as much support as possible regarding the use of this app, the developer shall not be responsible for any damages arising from its use.

Changes to Privacy Policy

This policy may be revised as necessary. After revision, it will be promptly published on this page.
